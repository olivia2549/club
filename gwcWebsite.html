Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a free community for all Johns Creek girls to learn how to code from the very start. This year, we are learning html, scratch, and python. For those who already know how to code, never fear! We work on many choice impact projects throughout the year -- both individually and in teams -- and are even setting up dates to teach Autrey Mill students as we learn. This is a very collaborative environment and a great chance to be social and creative at the end of a long day.
